Custom Jumborton

Using a series of utilites, you can create this jumborton, just like the one in previous versions of Bootstap. Check out the example below for how you can remix and restyle it to your liking.

Change the background

Swap the background-color utility and add a '.text-* color utility to mix up the jumborton look. Then, mix and watch with additional component themes additional more.

Add borders

Or, keep it light and add a border for some added definition to the boundaries of your content.
